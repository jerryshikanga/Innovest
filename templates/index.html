    About Us


    Smart ideas are born every day, their bright minds behind them spending hours on end to perfect them. However, Many a times these ideas fail to take of. One of the mahor cause for this is insufficiency of resources.


    The Innovest Team, having faced this challenge, brainstormed and came up with a platform which will make it easier for innovators to get their ideas funded easily. However, we are not just a crowd funding platform, our aim is to create a platform like the stock exchange where investors continually benefit from their investments. This comes with one advantage; Unlike the stocks there is no minimum or maximum limit on what you can invest! Its a free market.


    Our Values


    MISSION: Our mission is to connect innovators and investors not only in Kenya but all over the world.


    VISION: Our vision is an open market where investors and innovators can interact and trade without restriction.
